How Does DAROUBAR Works?

1. Your Prescription

Send us your prescription. We will recommend it to the drug stores and give you an estimate of how much you should pay.

2. Response

A drugstore will accept your prescription. then you will know the amount you have to pay.

3. Pay Up

You have two options to pay for the drugs: give it to delivery or do it online.

4. Delivery

After you choose how to pay, your drugs will be there in no time with our delivery bikes!
